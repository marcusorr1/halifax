Mobile banking

If you’re already registered for our Internet Banking services, your logon details will also work for Mobile Banking.

If you haven’t yet registered for Internet Banking, you can set up your details from your mobile device or go to .

Once you’ve done this, you’ll be able to use those details to log on to both Mobile and Internet Banking.

With Mobile Banking you’ll be able to check your balances, credit limits and a history of transactions on your Lloyds TSB UK personal current and savings accounts and credit card accounts.

Mobile Banking also lets you carry out some of the most common banking transactions on your mobile device:
- Transfer money between accounts.
- Set up new recipients and make payments.
- Request an extension to an existing Planned Overdraft.

You’ll also be able to change the details we have for you.
