Mobile Banking app

For iPhone and Android users

With Mobile Banking, you can carry out many everyday banking tasks on the go. Our app is fast, easy to use and very secure. With it you can:

  • Sign in quickly and securely using your memorable information
  • View the full transaction history on your eligible personal accounts
  • Manage your standing orders and direct debits on the move
  • View pending transactions and cheques being processed
  • Transfer funds between your accounts
  • Pay people and bills – and pay your phone contacts using just their mobile number
  • Find your nearest branch or ATM
  • If you’ve registered for Cashback Extras, you’ll be able to view and activate offers.

What you'll need

To use our Mobile Banking app, you'll need:

  • An iPhone running iOS 6.0 or above OR an Android device running 2.3 or above. You won't be able to use the app if we think your device has been tampered with (including jailbroken or rooted), and the app doesn't yet work on Intel based Android devices
  • An up-to-date phone number that you've registered with us, so we can reach you while you register your device
  • Data access on your device (either Wi-Fi or cellular)
  • An Apple App Store or Google Play account.

For Windows users

For a Windows device running 7.5 or above, our app is free to download from the Windows Phone Store. 

Don’t have a compatible device?

We recommend you bookmark the previous log on page for Mobile Banking. Please visit the relevant manufacturer's website for more information about bookmarking.
